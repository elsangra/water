    • Partner with Us

    Water 10,000 is a project aimed at providing access to clean and safe water to 10,000 rural households in Kenya. The project is a joint initiative of Kenya's Ministry of Water, Sanitation, and Irrigation, and various other partners. We invite you to partner with us and help us achieve our goal of providing access to clean water to every Kenyan household.

    Benefits of Partnering with Us

    • Make a significant impact on the lives of thousands of Kenyan families.
    • Contribute to the development of Kenya's water and sanitation sector.
    • Showcase your commitment to corporate social responsibility.
    • Gain positive brand exposure through our marketing and publicity campaigns.

    Our Current Partners

    Our project is already being supported by some of the most respected organizations in the world, including:

    • The World Bank
    • The United Nations Development Programme (UNDP)
    • The Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation
    • The African Development Bank
